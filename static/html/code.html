\\WHO AM I?

Hi, I’m Aidan - frontend UI/UX enthusiast and high school student. I’m a software developer based in Boulder, Colorado, primarily experienced in frontend web development. I grew up in Longmont, Colorado and have lived here my entire life, starting my code career with Harvard’s online CS50 Course my freshman summer. I’ve been writing code for fun as well as for Up-A-Creek Robotics – a highly competitive robotics team that has consistently competed at a world-class level for years now – for around two years now. Over that time, I’ve developed a scouting app – encountering things like API calls, dependencies, and many other pillars of web development for the first time – and landed an internship called Computing for the Good at Colorado School of Mines. The spark has not died out, and it seems like it won’t anytime soon. Outside of code, I run cross country, track, and generally try to spend as much time outside as possible.
